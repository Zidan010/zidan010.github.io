I am a Machine Learning Engineer at Nasir Syntax Solution Limited in Dhaka, Bangladesh, working on production RAG and agentic LLM systems, LoRA/QLoRA fine-tuning, and computer vision pipelines for object detection, segmentation, and 3D reconstruction. I hold a BSc in Computer Science & Engineering from American International University Bangladesh (AIUB), where my thesis applied classical machine learning algorithms to mental health condition prediction.

My research interests center on two threads: large language models — fine-tuning, agentic systems, and retrieval-augmented generation — and computer vision, particularly efficient monocular depth estimation and edge-deployable models. I am currently a contributing author on a paper applying explainable machine learning (SHAP, LIME) to five years of COVID-19 vaccine adverse event data (under review, AIUB Journal of Science and Engineering), and co-developing a lightweight self-supervised depth estimation network for mobile and edge devices.

I am seeking fully funded, thesis-based PhD positions starting Fall 2027 (with select Fall 2026 opportunities), in areas spanning efficient deep learning, applied XAI, and LLM/agentic systems. You can reach me at [sazidan559@gmail.com](mailto:sazidan559@gmail.com).
