A self-supervised monocular depth estimation network replacing the standard ResNet-18 encoder with a MobileNetV2 backbone, paired with a custom Mobile-Aware Depth Decoder using cross-gated feature fusion and learned scale calibration, targeting real-time inference on mobile and edge hardware.
