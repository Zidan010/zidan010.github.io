A dual-model explainable machine learning system trained on 1,052,335 COVID-19 vaccine adverse event records from VAERS (2019–2026). An XGBoost severity classifier achieves 0.8498 AUC-ROC and 0.81 Fatal-class recall; a neural network symptom co-occurrence model reaches 93.79% Top-3 accuracy. SHAP and LIME provide patient-level explanations for post-vaccination safety surveillance.
